About Chef China

Chef China is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving the San Diego community since 1999. Our mission is to provide authentic Chinese cuisine made with fresh ingredients, generous portions, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Whether you're dining in or catering for a large party, we are committed to delivering quality dishes that leave a lasting impression. Thank you for supporting our small business!
